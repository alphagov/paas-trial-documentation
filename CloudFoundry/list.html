Here's a list of things to try out:

While you're experimenting with the platform we'd love for you to try to perform some (or all) of these tasks:

  • Deploy an application
  • Bind an application to a backing service (Postgres / Redis)
  • Scale the number of instances of your app (up and down)
  • Connect to your application from the outside world to ensure it works
  • Drive traffic to your application (if you have the tools to do this)
  • Perform a zero-downtime deploy of a new version of your app (for bonus points try this with traffic being driven to the app at the same time)
  • Remove your app

If something goes wrong

If you need help or something goes wrong in the environment then feel free to contact us. We have a support email: support@governmentpaas.zendesk.com. We will aim to resolve all tickets within 4 business hours - let us know how we do.
